Project Zenith is a developing student organisation aiming to develop a Light multi-modal UAV platform based in Milano-Bovisa

To achieve this goal, we will bring students from multiple universities across Europe together and bridge our knowledge. 

# Kestrel 

This First initiative is a  medium-sized UAV currently in development. 

## Specifications: 
<img width="1695" height="1139" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b0996b3d-3d72-458c-ab1d-71be1e9c9028" />

- Main wing profile: Naca 4412

- V-tail wing profile: RAF 27

- Estimated Mass range: 3-5 kg

- Estimated Speed range: $(25-40) kms^{-1}$

- Uses carbon fiber spars to reinforce the wing.
  - two 12mmx500 mm
  - four 6mmx500mm
  - six 3mmx200mm
- The body of the plane will be printed out of LW PLA if possible to reduce weight.
